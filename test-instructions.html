🚀 How to Test Your QR Attendance System

✅ Your system is running at: http://localhost:8000

📋 Step-by-Step Testing Instructions:

Step 1: Generate a QR Code

  1. Go to http://localhost:8000
  2. Click the "Generate QR Code" button
  3. A QR code will appear with student information
  4. Take a screenshot of this QR code (or use your phone to take a photo)

Step 2: Scan the QR Code

  1. Go to http://localhost:8000/scanner/
  2. Enter your name in the "Scanner Name" field (this is who is scanning)
  3. Optionally enter a location (e.g., "Main Office", "Front Desk")
  4. Click "Choose File" and select your QR code screenshot
  5. Click "🔍 Scan QR Code"

Step 3: View Results

  1. After scanning, you should see success message with student and scanner info
  2. Go to http://localhost:8000/records/ to see all records
  3. Click Download Excel to get the Excel file

🔍 What Should Happen:

  • Student Info: Name: M.Abinaya, Department: Bsc.CS, Year: 3rd year
  • Scanner Info: Your name + location + current date/time
  • Excel File: Downloads automatically with all the information

🎯 Quick Navigation:

  • 🏠 Home (QR Generator)
  • 📱 QR Scanner
  • 📊 View Records
  • 📥 Download Excel

❗ Important Notes:

  • Scanner Name is Required: You must enter your name before scanning
  • Clear Images: Make sure QR code is clear and not blurry
  • One Scan Per Day: Each student can only be scanned once per day
